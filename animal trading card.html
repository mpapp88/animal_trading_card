Giant Panda

Pandas are BIG eaters – every day they fill their tummies for up to 12 hours, shifting up to 12 kilograms of bamboo!

  • Scientific name: Ailuropoda melanoleuca, which means “black and white cat-foot”.
  • Habitat: Remote, mountainous regions of central China, in Sichuan, Shaanxi and Gansu provinces.
  • Average Lenght: Between 1.2m and 1.5m.
  • Average Lifespan: 30 years.

Pandas are probably the most easily recognized bear in the world. Their fur is starkly contrasting patches of black and white, with black ears and black eye patches. Sadly, these beautiful bears are endangered, and it’s estimated that only around 1,000 remain in the wild. That’s why we need to do all we can to protect them!
